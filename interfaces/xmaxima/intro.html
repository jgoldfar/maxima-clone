Introduction to Maxima

Maxima is a computer program for doing mathematical calculations, symbolic manipulations, numerical computations and graphics. Procedures can be programmed and then run by Maxima to do complex tasks. Much of the syntax for other computer algebra systems such as Maple was copied from Maxima.
